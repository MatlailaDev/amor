About Amor

Where beauty meets passion, and every treatment is an expression of love.

At Amor, we believe that beauty is more than skin deep—it's a feeling, an experience, and a form of self-love. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create a sanctuary where clients can escape, rejuvenate, and discover their most radiant selves.

Our team of certified professionals brings years of expertise in makeup artistry, skincare, and wellness treatments. We're committed to using only the finest products and techniques to ensure you receive the exceptional service you deserve.
